Turkey paninis are the unsung heroes
of the sandwich world.

Stacked with lean protein to keep you full and fueled, melty cheese for that crave-worthy comfort, and tucked between golden, crispy bread that always hits just right. Throw in some greens or a punchy spread, and boom—it's a whole vibe. Warm, satisfying, and low-key nutritious? That’s a lunch-time flex.

TURKEY PANINI RECIPE

1.Start by prepping your sandwich ingredients: wash and dry a few leaves of lettuce, slice a ripe tomato into medium-thick rounds, and get your turkey and cheese ready.

2.Lightly toast two slices of whole grain or multigrain bread for a bit of crunch and warmth. Once toasted, spread mayonnaise on one or both slices—add mustard too if you’re feeling it.

3.Layer on the turkey breast slices generously, followed by the cheese. If you like your cheese melted, you can quickly toast or microwave the sandwich open-faced before adding the veggies.

4.Next, place the lettuce and tomato slices on top of the turkey and cheese. Season the tomato with a pinch of salt and pepper to boost flavor. Add any extras like pickles or red onion if you’d like, then close the sandwich with the second slice of bread.

5.Press it down slightly, slice it in half for that café-style finish, and you’re ready to go. It’s a super easy, satisfying, protein-packed sandwich—perfect for a quick lunch or chill picnic bite.
